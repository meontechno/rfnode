1. Prepare Your Files To Upload

To get maximum refunds please scan and upload your 3 years tax returns and w2's. If the IRS finds mistakes like a math error or missing schedule before you do, you will get a notice. The notice will tell you about the error and what information you need to submit to the IRS to correct it. See Incorrect Return for more information. With notices, there are usually other ways to correct errors besides an amended return.
